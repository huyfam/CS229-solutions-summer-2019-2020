Using the result \eqref{res2}, we then have:
\begin{eqnarray}
	\mathbb{E}[(l'(\eta))^2] &=& - \mathbb{E}[l''(\eta)] \\
	\mathbb{E}[(Y - a'(\eta))^2] &=& - \mathbb{E}[-a''(\eta)] \\
	\mathbb{E}[(Y - \mathbb{E}[Y])^2] &=& a''(\eta) \text{ (replace $a'(\eta)$ as expectation)} \\
	\text{Var}(Y) &=& a''(\eta)
